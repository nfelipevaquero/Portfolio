G A M E

LORE

Charlotte wakes up in the mysterious world of dans l'ombre, a strange dimension inhabited by anthropomorphic creatures that look like intelligent ducks. She is the only human, and this difference makes her feel even more disoriented. With no memories of who she is or how she got there, Charlotte embarks on a journey through varied landscapes, from enchanted forests to mysterious ruins, to recover the fragments of her memory.



Dans l'ombre interactive map

CHARACTERS



CHARLOTTE


She is on a journey to rediscover the pieces of her forgotten past. As she navigates through her lost records and false truths, she faces the challenge of reconnecting with who she is going to be and discovering who she is destined to be.


SQUACK


Charlottes' best Friend and best DUCK??!! Charlotte has the most amazing best friend in the world, but what makes this friendship even more unique is that her best friend just happens to be the most adorable, clever, and loyal duck you could ever meet!

LISETTE


A friend that Charlotte had. According to her parents, she went to Madrid to finish her studies, but something tells her that she has been away for too many years...





PLAYABILITY

Charlotte won’t be alone in her adventure. Squawk, a creature that has the sense of observation, will be her faithful company. Squawk helps Charlotte by providing information about enemies, and will be essential to overcome obstacles and discover the world’s hidden secrets.


In exchange for Charlotte's help, Squawk hopes that she will manage to save the life of his own, causing an extra layer of tension and collaboration between the two.
